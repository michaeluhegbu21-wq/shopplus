Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept payment upon Delivery to avoid some crisis.Cash or Transfer

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping is available for an additional fee and delivers in 2-3 business days. National shipping times vary by location.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all unused products in original packaging. Return shipping is free for defective items. Please contact our support team to initiate a return.

Do you offer shipping to all the states in Nigeria?

Yes, we ship to all 36 state in Nigeria. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination. You can calculate shipping costs at checkout.

What warranty do your products come with?

All products come with a 12-month manufacturer's warranty. Extended warranty options are available for select items at checkout.
